Our Story

Mug and Mingle Coffee began as a dream of creating a community space where people could gather, relax, and enjoy exceptional coffee. Founded by coffee enthusiasts with a passion for perfect brews and a cozy atmosphere, we opened our doors in the heart of our beloved Manchester.
Our journey started with a simple idea: to offer a warm, welcoming place where everyone feels at home, whether they're grabbing a quick coffee on the go or settling in for a long chat with friends.

Our Values and Mission

  • Quality: Sourcing the finest beans and ingredients to ensure every drink is perfect.
  • Sustainability: Implementing eco-friendly practices and supporting local suppliers.
  • Community: Creating a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.
  • Innovation: Constantly exploring new flavors and brewing methods to delight our customers.
